Awards & Accomplishments

National Awards

Shashika has won the first place on Thurunu Shakthi National Awards and the Yawwana Sammana National Awards

Raigam Tele'es Award

She won the Best Femal Vocalist 2007 for the work of Sedona and 2022 for Pichchamala Suwandai

Sumathi Awards

She won the Best Female Vocalist 2008 for the work of Sadagala Thanna and 2013 for Monara Thanna. And she won the Best Teledrama Singer Award 2020 for Pulingu

State Television Awards

She won the Best Female Vocalist 2009 for the work of a Theme song and 2012 for Sandagiri Pawwa.

State Music Awards

She won the Best Female Vocalist 2014 for the work of Me Tharam Digu Ai Rathriya and 2016 for a Theme song

SLIM-Kantar Peoples Awards

She has won the Most Popular Songstress Award for the past 8 consecutive years from 2016-2023
